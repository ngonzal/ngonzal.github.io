Nathan R. Gonzalez

Hi, My name is Nathan! I am a PhD student studying physics at the University of California, Davis. I am a member of the Radulaski Lab where I work on topics in quantum optics and nanophotonics.

My specific research interests are currently in developing near term analog quantum simulations of interesting physical phenomena by leveraging light-matter interactions in cavity quantum electrodynamics (CQED) systems. I am also interested in the development of in using photonics to bridge the gap between quantum computing and quantum communication.
